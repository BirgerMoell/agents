# ASCII palettes (ASCII-only)

## Shading ramps
- Light to dark (classic):
  - ` .:-=+*#%@`
- Alternative:
  - ` .'`^\",:;Il!i~+_-?][}{1)(|\\/tfjrxnuvczXYUJCLQ0OZmwqpdbkhao*#MW&8%B@$`
  (Use subsets; full ramp can be hard to read.)

## Outline sets
- Thin: `.-_`
- Structural: `=/\\|()`
- Corners/edges: `<>[]{}'

## Patterns
- Dither: alternating `.` and `:` or `.` and `,`
- Crosshatch: `xX` or `/#`

## Tips
- Prefer fewer characters for clarity in small art.
- Use spaces intentionally; they are part of the image.
